## Hints

1. You have either deleted the second `fieldset` element or it is missing an opening tag or closing tag.
2. Your `legend` element should have an opening tag. Opening tags have this syntax: `<elementName>`.
3. Your `legend` element should have a closing tag. Closing tags have a `/` just after the `<` character.
4. The `legend` element should have the text `What's your cat's personality?`. You have either omitted the text or have a typo.
