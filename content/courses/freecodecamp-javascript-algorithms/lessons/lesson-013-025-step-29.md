## Hints

1. You should have a `console.log()` call inside your nested `for` loop.
2. You should pass `array` as the first argument to `console.log()`.
3. You should pass `array[j]` as the second argument to `console.log()`.
4. You should pass `array[minIndex]` as the third argument to `console.log()`.
5. You should have an `if` statement after your `console.log()` call.
6. You should have a condition in your `if` statement that checks if `array[j]` is less than `array[minIndex]`.
7. Your `if` statement should set `minIndex` to `j`.
