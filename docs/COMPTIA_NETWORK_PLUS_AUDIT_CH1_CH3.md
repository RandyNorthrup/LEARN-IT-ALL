- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Description:** All lesson References sections and body text reference the **CompTIA Network+ N10-008** exam. The current exam version as of March 2026 is **N10-009** (released December 2024). Students using this material may be studying stale exam objectives.
- **Scope:** Every lesson in the audit (001-030). At least 50+ references to "N10-008" across all files.
- **Examples:**
  - [lesson-001-osi-model-overview.md](../content/courses/comptia-network-plus/lessons/lesson-001-osi-model-overview.md#L457): `CompTIA Network+ N10-008 Exam Objectives`

#### M3. Content Overlap Between Lessons
- **Severity:** MEDIUM
- **Description:** Some topics are covered in multiple lessons, creating redundancy:
  - **VLANs and STP** are introduced in lesson-003 (Layer 2 Data Link) AND have dedicated lessons (014, 016). The introductions in lesson-003 are brief enough to serve as previews, but the overlap could confuse students.
  - **MAC addressing** covered in detail in both lesson-003 AND lesson-011 (Ethernet Fundamentals).
  - **Routing fundamentals** overlaps between lesson-004 (Layer 3 Network) and lesson-017 (Routing Fundamentals).
- **Impact:** Not a bug — some overlap is pedagogically useful (spiral learning). However, cross-references should be added.
- **Fix:** Add explicit cross-references: "For a deeper dive into VLANs, see Lesson 14" etc.
